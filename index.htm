About Me

I am a Ph.D. Candidate in the Institute of Automation, Chinese Academy of Sciences (CASIA) advised by Prof. Zengguang Hou (IEEE Fellow) and Prof. Jian Cheng. Before that, I received my bachelor's degree from Central South Unversity (CSU) in 2015. I am a visiting scholar in Computer Science at the Queen Mary University of London (QMUL), advised by Prof. Shaogang Gong for 12 months from September 2019.

My research interest includes machine learning and pattern recognition, with a focus on Person Re-Identification, Hashing, Generative Adversarial Nets.
